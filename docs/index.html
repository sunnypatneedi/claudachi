Claudachi

A pocket-sized virtual pet for kids.
Lives on a card-sized computer. No screens, no apps, no ads.

Get started ↓
🛒
Step 1

Get a Cardputer-Adv

About $30. Make sure it says "Adv" version — the original Cardputer (no Adv) takes different firmware and won't work with this.

Step 2

Plug it in

Use a USB-C data cable (not the charging-only kind that came with your toothbrush). The Cardputer-Adv only enumerates one cable orientation — if nothing happens when you click below, flip the cable 180° and try again.

💻 📱
⚡
Step 3

Install firmware

Click below. The browser will ask for permission to talk to the Cardputer, then walk you through the button dance.

⚠️ Your browser doesn't support WebSerial. Use Chrome, Edge, or Brave on a desktop. ⚠️ WebSerial requires HTTPS.
What's the button dance?

Two flush-mounted buttons on the back of the Cardputer-Adv (a fingernail helps):

  1. HOLD the G0 button
  2. Still holding G0, TAP the RST button
  3. Release RST first
  4. Wait a second, then release G0
  5. Screen goes dark = ready to flash 🎯

Takes about 3 minutes after the dance — UIFlow firmware is ~8 MB and we flash slow-and-steady to be reliable.

📦
Step 4

Install Claudachi

After the firmware finishes and the device reboots, click below. The browser asks for the serial port one more time, then pushes the Claudachi pet (plus a few bonus apps) onto the device. ~30 seconds.

Waiting…
What does this push?

~150 KB of MicroPython into /flash/: Claudachi the pet, plus Snake, a Hello demo, and the Claude Buddy BLE client (a separate experimental app). Nothing leaves your machine.

🎉
Step 5

Have fun with Claudachi!

Power-cycle the Cardputer with the side switch. The launcher menu shows up — pick claudachi and meet your new friend.

Ffeed
Pplay
Znap
Ttalk
Qback

Each action plays a little chirp tune. Claudachi never dies, never asks personal questions, and runs entirely on the device — nothing online, nothing cloud-based, no parent worry.
